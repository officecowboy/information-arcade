Information Arcade

Artifacts from the World Wide Web

The Information Arcade is a collection of in-house projects, experiments, and souvenirs from the team at RRC. Some ideas are more developed than others. We hope you enjoy!

You can learn more about RRC and our work at recyclereality.net. The name "Information Arcade" is a tribute to the University of Iowa Library's 1992 initiative to bring electronic resources into research, teaching, and independent learning.
